# Week 1 Data Analyst Log

## What I Analyzed

This week, I reviewed InGen Dynamics' physical AI and robotics platforms and began framing each product from a data analyst perspective.

## What I Found

The main analytical focus is to understand which sensor or operational variables can explain platform performance, user interaction quality, or fault risk.

## Key Insight

The Aido Rover platform is especially suitable for early data analysis because it can be represented through telemetry data such as battery state of charge, LiDAR distance, wheel torque, GPS position, ambient temperature, and fault labels.

## Tsinghua Research Connection

This is similar to my previous multivariate regression research on Shanghai rental prices. In that project, the goal was to identify key determinants of rental price. In this internship, the goal is to identify key determinants of robot fault probability or platform performance.
